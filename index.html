Mission

The Union for Reform Chinese Culture (URCC) seeks to reform Chinese culture to accommodate progressive and democratic values using radical centrism,https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Radical_centrism

 with reasoning based on the classical and contemporary Chinese cultural canon, to benefit the Chinese diaspora and all people of Chinese culture.

URCC Manual of Style

  1. The website formatting shall be based in large part on https://edwardtufte.github.io/tufte-css/, which is Dave Liepmann’s adaptation of Edward Tufte’s style.

  2. The text formatting shall be loosely based on the latest Chicago Manual of Style (CMOS).https://www.chicagomanualofstyle.org/home.html

    .

  3. Where appropriate, for the purpose of increasing readability, with the consideration of the overwhelming number of website citations, the CMOS citation style may be simplified.

  4. In quoting from a website, the first few words of a paragraph may be included while italicized, to reference the appropriate section of the website. This is to remedy the lack of page numbers and paragraph numbers on a website.

  5. If the context is enough to identify the exact location of the source on a website, the footnote or sidenote may be omitted. Users can use the browser’s search function to locate the source quote.
